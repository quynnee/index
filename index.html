About us


It’s been nearly a decade since our Group was founded in January 2013!

Right from the beginning, it was the love for animals that prompted us, the youth generation, to voluntarily work together as a group. Overcoming ups and downs, we have endeavored to make strides in developing our Group so far.

At this time, we’re proud to be one of the most prestigious animal charities in Central Vietnam. Our Group works professionally, with more than 100 volunteers collaborating dependently, seriously and profoundly in different teams.
